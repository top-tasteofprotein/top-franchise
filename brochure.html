Real return (XIRR) vs other options
XIRR is the true yearly return on your money over the full 3 years. It counts the slow start, every monthly payout, and the equipment you still own at the end. It's the fair way to compare against an FD or a mutual fund.

06If Things Go Slow

We planned for
the bad months too.

You own real things
₹8.75L of equipment and deposit are yours from day one. Something solid, not just a promise.
₹15K floor
After the 3-month settling-in, your monthly payout never drops below ₹15,000.
We take the early hit
The first few months don't make money. We carry that cost, not you.
We buy it back^
If you haven't earned back your ₹12L by the end of the 3-year lock-in, we buy the kitchen back from you.

This suits you if

You want a hands-off investment you can hold for 3+ years.

You like steady monthly income more than quick gains.

Not for you if

You need quick returns or money you can pull out any time.

You want to run the kitchen yourself.

^ Buyback applies after the lock-in is complete and as per the terms in the signed franchise agreement.
